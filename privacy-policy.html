Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how G2G Church App ("G2G," "we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile and/or web-based application (the "App") and all associated services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you create an account, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, and other contact information.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our App, including pages visited, features used, and time spent on the App.
  • Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Location Data: With your consent, we may collect location information to provide location-based services.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our App and services.
  • To process transactions and send related information.
  • To send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support messages.
  • To respond to your comments, questions, and customer service requests.
  • To communicate with you about products, services, and events offered by G2G.
  • To monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our App.
  • To send one-time SMS text message notifications related to account creation (see Section 3 below).

3. SMS/Text Messaging Policy

What SMS Messages We Send

The G2G Church App may send a one-time SMS text message to new users when a church administrator or authorized family member creates an account on their behalf. This message contains app download links (for Google Play Store and Apple App Store) and login instructions to help the new user access their account.

How Consent is Obtained

When a church administrator or authorized family member creates an account for a new user through the G2G Church App, they are presented with a "Notify User About Their New Account" checkbox. This checkbox is unchecked by default. The administrator or family member must actively check this box to authorize sending a one-time SMS notification to the new user. The administrator or family member is responsible for obtaining the new user's verbal or written consent before enabling this notification.

Message Frequency

Users will receive a maximum of one (1) SMS message per account creation event. This is a one-time notification, not a recurring messaging program.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Please contact your mobile carrier for details about your text messaging plan.

Opt-Out

You may opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message received from G2G Church App. After opting out, you will receive a confirmation message and will no longer receive SMS messages from us.

Help

For help with SMS messaging, reply HELP to any message received from G2G Church App, or contact us at info@g2g.church.

Carrier Liability

Mobile carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Phone Number Privacy

Your phone number will not be shared with or sold to third parties for marketing purposes. Your phone number is collected solely for account creation and one-time notification purposes.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.
  • We may share information with service providers who assist us in operating our App.
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.
  • We may use aggregated, anonymized data for analytics and improvements.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. We may also retain and use your information to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

7. Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information through your account settings.
  • Opt-out of receiving promotional communications.
  • Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.

8. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
